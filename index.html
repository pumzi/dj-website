Who Am I?

I'm DJ Blanche Beats — a Miami-based vinyl junkie serving nostalgia on wax. Inspired by the glam and glitter of the 80s (and the sass of the Golden Girls), I mix classic disco with deep house and neon funk.

Whether it's beach parties or velvet lounges, I bring good vibes and pastel power wherever I go.
