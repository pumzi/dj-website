Who Am I?

I'm Slammer — a sound designer, audio producer, turntable evangelist and dance floor imperialist. I believe that the revolution has always been now despite their cynical demeanor and that building media democracy is a way of life not a hobby. You can find them roaming through the concrete jungle of Washington, D.C. and beyond with their DJ Crew, Anthology of Booty.

Whether it's beach parties or velvet lounges, I bring good vibes and pastel power wherever I go.
